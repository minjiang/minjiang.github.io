About me
Currently I am a professor of Department of Artificial Intelligence in Xiamen University. I received my B.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from Wuhan University in June 2001 and June 2007, respectively. From 2005 to 2007, I was a visiting student at Department of Computer Science of City university of Hong Kong. I am an IEEE Senior Member (MIEEE’201l, SMIEEE’2012).
